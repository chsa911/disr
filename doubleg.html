The Double G method

1. G = Get, means don t stick with the books you have but get books that you love and blow you away. Search for paperbooks online, on peddlars markets in bookshops wherever.

2. G = Get rid of = pages that you have read and the books that you don’t like. Select actively by reading a lot as there is no guarantuee that the books you purchased are what you are looking for and especially keep their quality to the end. As soon as you don’t like it anymore. Garbage can. Read another one or get a new one. Consider reading as a trial and error process to find your pageturners.
